This guide explains the role system in 7POINTS.NET, detailing the differences between coordinators and regular members, and how to manage roles within your apartment.

## Role Overview

Every apartment member has one of two roles:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Koordinatzailea" icon="shield-check" color="#6366f1">
    **Coordinator** - Full administrative privileges for the apartment
  </Card>
  <Card title="Normala" icon="user" color="#6b7280">
    **Regular Member** - Standard access to apartment features
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The role is stored in the `mota` field of the pivot table connecting users to apartments (pisos).

## Regular Member (Normala)

### Permissions

Regular members can:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Task Management" icon="list-check">
    - View all apartment tasks
    - Create new tasks
    - Edit tasks (including reassignment)
    - Delete tasks
    - Update task status (to-do, in-progress, completed)
    - Assign tasks to any apartment member
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Expense Management" icon="money-bill">
    - View all apartment expenses
    - Add new expenses
    - Edit existing expenses (any expense, not just their own)
    - Delete expenses
    - View debt calculations
    - Settle debts (mark payments as complete)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Member Access" icon="users">
    - View list of all apartment members
    - See member roles (coordinator vs regular)
    - See member join dates
    - View contact information
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Self-Management" icon="user-gear">
    - Leave the apartment voluntarily
    - Switch between multiple apartments (if member of several)
    - Update their own profile information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Restrictions

Regular members CANNOT:

- Edit apartment details (name, code)
- Delete the entire apartment
- Remove other members from the apartment
- Promote members to coordinator
- Access coordinator-specific settings

<Info>
Regular members have full access to day-to-day apartment management (tasks, expenses) but cannot modify apartment structure or membership.
</Info>

## Coordinator (Koordinatzailea)

### Permissions

Coordinators have ALL regular member permissions, plus:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Apartment Management" icon="building">
    - **Edit apartment details**: Change apartment name and code
    - **Delete apartment**: Permanently remove the entire apartment
    - Access apartment settings and configuration
    
    These options are available via the three-dot menu (⋮) on the members page.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Member Management" icon="user-shield">
    - **Remove members**: Kick any regular member from the apartment
    - **Promote members**: Grant coordinator status to regular members
    - View detailed member information
    
    Each member card shows a dropdown menu with these options (except for other coordinators).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Authority Transfer" icon="hand-holding-hand">
    - Transfer coordinator status to another member
    - When transferring, the current coordinator becomes a regular member
    - Only one coordinator can exist per apartment at a time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Important Coordinator Notes

<Warning>
Deleting an apartment is permanent and cannot be undone. All tasks, expenses, and member associations will be lost. Use this action with extreme caution.
</Warning>

<Note>
Coordinators cannot remove other coordinators from the apartment. The promoted coordinator must demote the previous one, or that person must leave voluntarily.
</Note>

## How Roles Are Assigned

### Automatic Assignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apartment Creation">
    When someone creates a new apartment:
    - They automatically become the coordinator
    - Role: `mota: 'koordinatzailea'`
    - This happens in `PisoController@store` (line 107)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Joining via Code">
    When someone joins using an apartment code:
    - They become a regular member
    - Role: `mota: 'normala'`
    - This happens in `PisoController@join` (line 42)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Manual Role Changes

Only coordinators can change roles through the members page.

## Promoting a Member to Coordinator

Coordinators can transfer their role to another member:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Members Page">
    Navigate to **Kideak** (Members) from the menu or go to `/pisua/kideak`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Member to Promote">
    Locate the member card for the person you want to promote.
    
    Regular members show a **"Kidea"** (Member) badge.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Member Menu">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) icon on the member's card.
    
    This menu only appears for regular members (not other coordinators).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Koordinatzaile egin'">
    Select **"Koordinatzaile egin"** (Make Coordinator) from the dropdown.
    
    A transaction occurs that:
    - Changes the selected member's role to `koordinatzailea`
    - Changes YOUR role to `normala`
    - Refreshes the page to reflect changes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Role Transfer Complete">
    The promoted member now has full coordinator permissions.
    
    You are now a regular member and can no longer manage the apartment structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Promoting someone to coordinator AUTOMATICALLY demotes you to a regular member. Only promote members you trust completely, as you'll lose administrative control.
</Warning>

## Removing a Member

Coordinators can remove regular members from the apartment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Members Page">
    Go to the Kideak (Members) page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate the Member">
    Find the member card for the person to remove.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Member Menu">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on their card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Pisotik kendu'">
    Select **"Pisotik kendu"** (Remove from apartment).
    
    Confirm the action when prompted:
    **"Ziur zaude erabiltzaile hau pisotik bota nahi duzula?"**
    (Are you sure you want to remove this user from the apartment?)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Member Removed">
    The member is immediately removed:
    - Their access to the apartment is revoked
    - They're detached from the `pisua` relationship
    - If they were logged in, they'll lose access on next page load
    - They can rejoin later if they have the apartment code
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Removed members can rejoin using the apartment code if they still have it. To prevent this, change the apartment code after removing someone.
</Note>

## Leaving an Apartment Voluntarily

Any member (coordinator or regular) can leave an apartment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Members Page">
    Navigate to the Kideak page for the apartment you want to leave.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Atera pisutik'">
    At the bottom left of the page, click the **"Atera pisutik"** (Leave apartment) button.
    
    This button is visible to all members, including coordinators.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Departure">
    Confirm the action when prompted:
    **"Ziur zaude pisutik atera nahi duzula? Ekintza hau ezin da desegin."**
    (Are you sure you want to leave the apartment? This action cannot be undone.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Succession (Coordinators Only)">
    If you're the coordinator:
    - The system finds the OLDEST member (earliest `created_at` in pivot table)
    - That member is automatically promoted to coordinator
    - This ensures the apartment always has a coordinator
    
    If you're a regular member:
    - You simply leave, no succession needed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Session Cleared">
    After leaving:
    - Your apartment session is cleared
    - You're redirected to the dashboard
    - You can rejoin later using the code (if you have it)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
When a coordinator leaves, the longest-standing member automatically inherits the coordinator role. This prevents apartments from becoming "orphaned" without a coordinator.
</Info>

## Transferring Apartment Ownership

There are two ways to transfer apartment control:

### Method 1: Promote and Stay

1. Coordinator promotes another member
2. Both remain in the apartment
3. Original coordinator becomes a regular member
4. New coordinator has full control

### Method 2: Leave and Auto-Promote

1. Coordinator leaves the apartment
2. System automatically promotes the oldest member
3. Original coordinator is no longer a member
4. They can rejoin later as a regular member (if they have the code)

<Tip>
If you want to transfer ownership to a specific person, use Method 1 (promote them directly). Method 2 (leaving) transfers to the oldest member, which may not be who you want.
</Tip>

## Multiple Apartments and Roles

Users can be members of multiple apartments simultaneously:

- You might be a **coordinator** in one apartment
- And a **regular member** in another apartment
- Roles are independent per apartment
- Switch apartments using the apartment selection screen

<Note>
Your permissions change based on which apartment is currently active in your session.
</Note>

## Viewing Your Role

To check your role in the current apartment:

1. Go to the **Kideak** (Members) page
2. Find your own member card
3. Look for the badge:
   - **"Koordinatzailea"** with shield icon = Coordinator
   - **"Kidea"** with user icon = Regular Member

## Role-Based UI Changes

The interface adapts based on your role:

### Coordinators See:
- Three-dot menu (⋮) at top right of members page (apartment actions)
- Three-dot menu on each member card (member management)
- Edit and Delete apartment options
- Promote and Remove member options

### Regular Members See:
- No apartment management menu
- No member action menus
- Only the "Leave apartment" button for self-removal

<Info>
The UI uses the `isCurrentUserAdmin` prop (passed from backend) to conditionally render coordinator-only features.
</Info>

## Security and Authorization

### Backend Protection

Role permissions are enforced at multiple levels:

1. **Route Middleware**: All apartment routes require authentication
2. **Policy Gates**: Coordinator actions use Laravel's Gate authorization
   - `Gate::authorize('update', $pisua)` checks coordinator status
   - `Gate::authorize('delete', $pisua)` for destructive actions
3. **Controller Checks**: Additional manual verification in controller methods

### Key Security Points

<Warning>
Never rely on frontend UI hiding alone for security. All coordinator actions are verified in the backend:

- Edit apartment: Checks `Gate::authorize('update')` (line 128, 134)
- Delete apartment: Checks `Gate::authorize('delete')` (line 160)
- Promote member: Checks `Gate::authorize('update')` (line 225)
- Remove member: Checks `Gate::authorize('update')` (line 245)
</Warning>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can there be multiple coordinators?">
    No. The current system design allows only ONE coordinator per apartment.
    
    When you promote someone, you automatically become a regular member.
    
    For shared administration:
    - Coordinate decisions in person
    - Transfer coordinator role temporarily when needed
    - Or have one coordinator consult others before actions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if the coordinator abandons the apartment?">
    If the coordinator stops using the app but doesn't formally leave:
    - They retain coordinator status indefinitely
    - Other members cannot promote themselves
    - Members can leave and create a new apartment
    - Or contact support to resolve the issue
    
    There's no automatic timeout or inactivity demotion.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I remove myself as a regular member?">
    Yes! Regular members can remove themselves using the "Atera pisutik" (Leave apartment) button.
    
    This is different from coordinators removing members - you're removing yourself voluntarily.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can a coordinator remove themselves without leaving?">
    Not directly. To step down as coordinator:
    
    **Option 1**: Promote another member (you become regular)
    **Option 2**: Leave the apartment (oldest member becomes coordinator)
    
    You cannot demote yourself to regular while staying without promoting someone else.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens to tasks/expenses if a member is removed?">
    - Tasks assigned to removed members remain in the database
    - Expenses paid by removed members remain in records
    - Debt calculations still include their contributions
    - You may need to manually reassign tasks or adjust expenses
    
    The system does NOT automatically clean up data from removed members.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Implementation

### Role Storage
- **Field**: `mota` in the `pisua_user` pivot table
- **Values**: `'koordinatzailea'` or `'normala'`
- **Relationship**: Many-to-many between `users` and `pisua` tables

### Key Controller Methods
- **Promote**: `PisoController@promoteMember` (line 219)
- **Remove**: `PisoController@removeMember` (line 240)
- **Leave**: `PisoController@atera` (line 262)
- **Auto-succession**: In `atera()` method (lines 279-290)

### Authorization
- **Policy**: Likely defined in `app/Policies/PisoPolicy.php`
- **Gate Check**: `Gate::authorize('update', $pisua)` verifies coordinator status
- **Implementation**: Checks if authenticated user's pivot `mota == 'koordinatzailea'`

### UI Detection
```php
// Backend: app/Http/Controllers/PisoController.php:209-210
$currentUser = $pisua->inquilinos->find(auth()->id());
$isCurrentUserAdmin = $currentUser && $currentUser->pivot->mota === 'koordinatzailea';
```

  // Coordinator-only UI elements
