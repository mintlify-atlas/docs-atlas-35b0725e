## Profile Settings

Manage your basic account information including name and email.

### Accessing Profile Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click your profile icon in the top navigation bar and select **Settings** or go directly to `/settings/profile`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Profile Information">
    You'll see your current name and email address displayed in editable fields.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Updating Your Name

1. In the **Izena** (Name) field, enter your new name
2. Click **Gorde** (Save)
3. You'll see a "Gordeta" (Saved) confirmation message

<Note>
Your name is visible to your apartment roommates and appears in expense records and task assignments.
</Note>

### Updating Your Email

1. In the **Emaila** (Email) field, enter your new email address
2. Click **Gorde** (Save)
3. **Important**: If you change your email, your account will be marked as unverified

<Warning>
After changing your email, you must verify the new address by clicking the link sent to your new email. You may lose access to some features until verification is complete.
</Warning>

#### Email Verification

If your email is unverified, you'll see a message:

> "Your email address is unverified. Click here to resend the verification email."

Click the link to receive a new verification email. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.

### Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, apartment associations, and records will be deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scroll to Delete Section">
    At the bottom of the profile settings page, find the **Delete Account** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with Password">
    You must enter your current password to confirm deletion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete">
    Click the delete button. Your account will be immediately removed and you'll be logged out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Before deleting your account, consider:
- Leaving all apartments you're a member of
- Settling any outstanding debts
- Transferring coordinator roles if you're the apartment coordinator
</Info>

## Password Management

Change your password to maintain account security.

### Changing Your Password

Navigate to **Settings > Password** (`/settings/password`):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    For security, you must provide your current password first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Type your new password. It should be strong and unique.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm New Password">
    Re-type your new password to ensure accuracy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Update Password** to apply the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

Your password should:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols (recommended)
- Not be a commonly used password
- Not match your email address

<Tip>
Use a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
</Tip>

### Rate Limiting

<Note>
Password change attempts are rate-limited to 6 attempts per minute to prevent brute-force attacks. If you exceed this limit, wait one minute before trying again.
</Note>

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account with two-factor authentication.

### What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) requires two forms of verification when logging in:
1. **Something you know** - Your password
2. **Something you have** - A code from your phone or authenticator app

Even if someone obtains your password, they won't be able to access your account without the second factor.

### Setting Up 2FA

Navigate to **Settings > Two-Factor** (`/settings/two-factor`):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication">
    Click the **Enable** button on the two-factor authentication page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Use an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password) to scan the displayed QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Recovery Codes">
    You'll receive recovery codes. Store these securely - you'll need them if you lose access to your authenticator device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Setup">
    Enter a code from your authenticator app to confirm the setup is working correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Using 2FA to Log In

Once enabled, logging in requires two steps:

1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app when prompted

The code changes every 30 seconds, so enter it promptly.

### Recovery Codes

<Warning>
Store your recovery codes in a secure location. If you lose your authenticator device without recovery codes, you may permanently lose access to your account.
</Warning>

Recovery codes can be used instead of authenticator codes to log in. Each code can only be used once.

To use a recovery code:
1. On the 2FA login screen, click "Use a recovery code"
2. Enter one of your saved recovery codes
3. Log in and immediately regenerate new recovery codes

### Disabling 2FA

If you need to disable two-factor authentication:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Two-Factor Settings">
    Navigate to `/settings/two-factor`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Password">
    Enter your password to verify your identity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable">
    Click **Disable Two-Factor Authentication**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
We strongly recommend keeping 2FA enabled, especially if you're a coordinator managing apartment finances.
</Tip>

## Appearance Settings

Customize the visual theme of 7POINTS.NET to match your preferences.

### Accessing Appearance Settings

Navigate to **Settings > Appearance** (`/settings/appearance`).

### Theme Options

Choose between different visual themes:

- **Light Mode** - Bright interface with dark text on light backgrounds
- **Dark Mode** - Reduced eye strain with light text on dark backgrounds
- **System Default** - Automatically matches your device's theme settings

<Info>
Theme preferences are saved to your account and sync across all devices where you're logged in.
</Info>

### Changing Your Theme

1. Go to Appearance settings
2. Select your preferred theme (Light, Dark, or System)
3. The interface updates immediately - no save button needed

<Tip>
Dark mode is particularly useful when using 7POINTS.NET at night or in low-light environments.
</Tip>

## Account Security Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a Strong, Unique Password">
    Never reuse passwords across different websites. If one site is compromised, attackers can access your other accounts. Use a password manager to generate and store unique passwords.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication">
    2FA significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access. Even if your password is compromised, attackers still can't access your account without the second factor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep Your Email Secure">
    Your email is the key to password recovery. Use a strong, unique password for your email account and enable 2FA there as well.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verify Email Changes">
    Always verify your email immediately after changing it. Unverified emails can lock you out of important features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review Recovery Codes">
    Store 2FA recovery codes in a secure location separate from your authenticator device. Consider printing them and storing them in a safe place.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Log Out on Shared Devices">
    If you access 7POINTS.NET on a shared computer, always log out when finished to prevent unauthorized access.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Settings Navigation

All settings pages are accessible from the main settings menu:

| Setting | URL | Purpose |
|---------|-----|---------|
| Profile | `/settings/profile` | Edit name, email, delete account |
| Password | `/settings/password` | Change password |
| Two-Factor | `/settings/two-factor` | Enable/disable 2FA |
| Appearance | `/settings/appearance` | Change theme (light/dark mode) |

<Note>
All settings pages require you to be logged in. If your session expires, you'll be redirected to the login page.
</Note>

## Technical Details

### Profile Update API

**Endpoint**: `PATCH /settings/profile`

**Request Body**:
```json
{
  "name": "New Name",
  "email": "newemail@example.com"
}
```

**Response**: Redirects to profile edit page with success status

### Password Update API

**Endpoint**: `PUT /settings/password`

**Request Body**:
```json
{
  "current_password": "old_password",
  "password": "new_password",
  "password_confirmation": "new_password"
}
```

**Rate Limit**: 6 requests per minute (enforced via `throttle:6,1` middleware)

### Account Deletion API

**Endpoint**: `DELETE /settings/profile`

**Request Body**:
```json
{
  "password": "current_password"
}
```

**Effect**: 
- Logs out user
- Deletes user record (cascading deletes apartments if sole member)
- Invalidates session
- Redirects to homepage

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page. You'll receive an email with a password reset link. Click it to set a new password.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email verification link expired">
    Go to your profile settings. If your email is unverified, you'll see a link to resend the verification email. Click it to receive a new link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I lost my 2FA device">
    Use a recovery code to log in. Once logged in, go to two-factor settings to disable 2FA, then set it up again with a new device. If you don't have recovery codes, contact support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password change says 'current password incorrect'">
    Double-check that you're typing your current password correctly. Remember passwords are case-sensitive. If you've forgotten it, use the password reset feature instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't delete my account">
    Account deletion requires your correct password. If you've forgotten it, reset your password first, then try deletion again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Theme not changing">
    Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser. Try refreshing the page. If using System theme, check your operating system's theme settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
